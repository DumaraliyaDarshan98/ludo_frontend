Platform Commission

Platfrom commission is taken by each participant equally in every contest or battle. Commission of any battle/contest is shown to participant before joining the contest/battle.

Below 200 cash games

We charge flat 10% commission on each battle.

Above 200 & Below 500 cash games

We charge flat 25 coin commission on each battle.

Above 500 cash games

We charge flat 5% commission on each battle.
